About Me

Hello! I'm happy to see you here. My name is Garrett Brooks, and I am among other things a photographer. I’ve done work on genealogy in the past as well, but my main job currently is working in retail. I’m a college student at College of the Canyons majoring in communication, due to the fact that I have a lot of possible routes to go down. I’ve been taking photos for over three years with my DSLR, but I first started with my camera at the age of 7 with a small handheld Canon camera. I’ve been surrounded by cameras my entire life. Photography wise, I have done shoots with my friends for their LinkedIn and their Instagram, and I have also done a more professional shoot for a bridal party. As I do not have much experience so far with professional photography, my rates are cheaper than most which is great for you, the consumer, because that way you can get a good photographer for less!

My rates at this time are:

$60-75/hr

(depending on event, could be less - please inquire about booking!)

The longer I am at the event, the cheaper the overall price will be. For example, if you were to book me for a 6-hr long event, I will charge about $330. Depending on the event prices will vary, so this is not set in stone. It depends on what it is for, who you are, etc.

Basically what I want from you if you want to book me is communication. I major in it so it’s not a problem for me. I can work with a budget as well. The last time I did an event I had almost one thousand photos, many of which worked for the guest and was able to make lifelong memories out of the photos.

I also edit photos as well, depending on the circumstances.
